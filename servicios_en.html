Our range of services!

Dry-cleaned


 Dry cleaning is done with specific products that clean fabrics without the use of water with the same efficiency as conventional washing.

 There are specific fabrics that do not respond well to water, harming the material of the garment and that is why the importance of dry cleaning.

Traditional washing


 We always use high-tech products and equipment, in addition to the concern with a good aroma in our products.

 We review the clothes in detail to find out what type of washing is appropriate. We have the best washing resources and the best quality products.

Sewing

 In our sewing workshop we can offer the best solutions for your clothes, curtains, sheets and much more.

 With our experience placed in professional hands, we offer you the guarantee that your parts will be customized according to your needs.

Carpets

 Keep your rugs and carpets clean, cared for with our professional technology and cleaning products service.

 We do the complete extraction of mites, bacteria, fungus, germs and dust from your rugs and carpets using industrial machines to make them look like new.

Curtains


 We offer a professional curtain cleaning service, with the best products and equipment, removing stains, dirt, odors, mites and fungi.

 If it is necessary, we do the removal and assembly of the same.

Pet


 We clean everything related to your pet, from beds, sheets and clothes.

 We guarantee that the product will look like new, with no hair, no blemishes and a pleasant aroma.

¡Professionally washed par excellence, always to meet your requirements!
